Welcome to "Winmark Projects Pvt. Ltd."

Our Commitment to Excellence in Construction and Design

Established in 2020, Winmark Projects Pvt. Ltd. is built on a strong legacy rooted in our earlier ventures—Sanify Technologies India and Ahad Fabs & Co., founded in 2012. Ahad Fabs & Co. evolved into a dedicated construction and scaffolding manufacturing firm, gaining recognition for excellence in general contracting and as a reliable distributor of shuttering materials.
Over the years, we have earned a solid reputation for delivering top-quality work across a wide spectrum of industries. From individual residential buildings to large-scale commercial and industrial developments, our projects reflect our commitment to excellence, precision, and integrity. Today, Winmark Projects is recognized as a trusted contractor known for its expertise and unwavering dedication to fulfilling client expectations.
Our success is driven by a team of skilled professionals who are committed to delivering outstanding service—on time and within budget. With a wealth of experience and a deep understanding of the industry, we provide reliable, efficient, and tailored solutions for every project we undertake.
